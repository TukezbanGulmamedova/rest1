Mochi

Mochi (Japanese: 餅) is a Japanese type of rice dough. Mochi is made from glutinous rice crushed into a paste, a special variety of mochigome known for a long time, which, when crushed and soaked for a long time, acquires a sweet taste. This is also the name of the round flatbreads made from this dough.
